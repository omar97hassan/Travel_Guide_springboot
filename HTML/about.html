About our Project

Idea

The main idea of desired software is to offer easy to use and fast solution of planning the whole trip. For now not a single application or a website deals with every aspect of your trip planning, even though many of them claim that they do. This software should offer you the ability to select event that you are going to, and then present you with various information like expected weather, average accommodation costs and best options, landmarks that are recommended to visit while you are there and more.

Simmilar Apps

Booking.com

Very popular site that offers huge number of accommodation options in any price range, and any corner of the planet. What it lacks is that you are not informed about any relevant or nice to have information related to the area you are traveling to.

Google Trips

This application offers the most similar set of features to the above idea. By using the app you are able to plan your free time in any city you find yourself in, you are able to enter the information of your flight ticket and get reminders about it, it also offers navigation around the city. Downside is that your flight ticket info and accommodation reservation are entered manually and they serve more as an diary than a planning tool.

Foursquare

Offers only information about places to spend your free time, find places to eat and amuse yourself. No information about accomodation, wather.

What do we do better

This would be a grate companion app for any traveller. There are many people who are not able to properly plan they trips (for various reasons: tight schedules, emergency situations, etc.), but there are also people who don’t know how to plan it. Many of them rely on the apps that are already trying to offer similar solution, but instead of having one place to go they are forced to use number of different services.
